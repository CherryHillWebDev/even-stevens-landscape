Our Story

Started in Oaklyn. Built One Yard at a Time.

Even Steven's was founded in 2023 by Steve, a South Jersey native who traded the corporate world for something he actually loves doing. Steve holds a BBA and MBA from Rutgers, but what really got him into landscaping was simpler than that: he genuinely enjoys working outside, and he's good at it.

Steve started the business in Oaklyn and has been growing it steadily ever since, mostly through word of mouth from neighbors who liked the work and told someone about it. That's still how most of our new clients find us, and honestly that's the best kind of referral there is.

When you call Even Steven's, you're talking to Steve. When we show up to your property, Steve is there. There's no middleman, no crew you've never met, no wondering who's going to show up. That level of consistency is something the bigger companies just can't offer, and it's something Steve takes real pride in.

This year Steve is expanding into hardscaping and design, so Even Steven's can handle full outdoor transformations from the ground up. Same values, bigger toolkit.

What We Stand For

How We Think About Our Work

These aren't company slogans. They're the actual beliefs that shape how we run our crews, communicate with clients, and decide what to say yes or no to.

🤝

Honesty First

We tell you what your property actually needs — not what adds the most to an invoice. If something can wait or doesn't make sense for your situation, we'll say so.

📐

Quality Over Speed

We'd rather take an extra 20 minutes and leave your property looking sharp than rush through it and let something slide. The details matter, and our clients notice them.

📅

Reliability Is Non-Negotiable

We show up when we say we will. If something changes — weather, scheduling — we communicate proactively. You should never be guessing whether we're coming.

🏡

Community Matters

We live in Camden County. The neighborhoods we work in are our neighborhoods. We take that seriously — in the work we do and in how we treat the people we work for.

📞

Accessible and Responsive

You can call, text, or email us and get a real response — usually same day. We're not a big corporate operation and we don't act like one.

🌱

Respect for the Property

Whether we're mowing a lawn or installing a full landscape renovation, we work clean, protect what's there, and treat your property like we'd want ours treated.
