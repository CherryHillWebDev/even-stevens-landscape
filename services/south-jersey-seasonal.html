South Jersey Expertise You Can Feel

Seasonal service timing in South Jersey has a tighter window than most homeowners expect. Cherry Hill and Haddonfield typically see their last frost around mid-April — which means spring cleanup needs to happen in that narrow window before growth accelerates and before you lose the chance to get aeration and overseeding down at the right soil temperature. Miss it by two weeks and you're waiting until fall.

On the back end, fall leaf removal in Camden County is a two-act job. The early drop (maples, sweet gums) hits in October, but the heavy load from oaks doesn't finish until late November. We can schedule multiple fall visits to keep your lawn protected through the whole leaf season — leaving leaves packed on turf through November is one of the most common causes of lawn damage we see each spring.
