Services & Seasonal Schedule
We offer a full range of residential lawn care and landscaping services, including:

In South Jersey, lawn mowing typically runs from late March through late October — though the exact start depends on when your grass breaks dormancy that year. We monitor conditions locally rather than following a fixed calendar date. After mowing season, we continue operating year-round with fall cleanup, leaf removal, and winter property services so your property stays maintained through every season.
Our spring cleanup typically includes removing winter debris, clearing dead plant material from beds, edging borders, and preparing your lawn and landscape for the growing season. Our fall cleanup covers leaf removal, cutting back spent perennials, clearing beds, and getting your property neat and protected before winter sets in. The exact scope is tailored to your property — we'll assess what's needed during your free estimate visit.
We offer both. Many of our clients are on weekly or bi-weekly recurring maintenance schedules, but we also take on one-time cleanups, mulch installs, and seasonal projects.
